Diverge Support

If you need help with Diverge, have a bug report, or want to send feedback, use the contact details below.

Email: contactdivergeus@gmail.com
Response time: Usually within 1–2 business days.

Common help topics

  • App crashes or won’t open.
  • Account or sign-in issues.
  • Missing data or sync problems.
  • Billing or subscription questions.
  • Feature requests and feedback.

Before contacting support

  • Update to the latest version of Diverge.
  • Restart your device.
  • Include your device model and iOS version.
  • Describe what you were doing when the issue happened.
